About Us

Established in 2015 as a wholly owned Ghanaian Company, Berton GOC Co. Ltd is a leading provider of Facility Management and MEP Services in Ghana. With a wealth of experience in the industry, we specialize in various areas such as:
• Building Systems Management
• Soft and hard facility Management
• Electromechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing Technical installations

Mission Statement

At Berton GOC, our mission is to provide exceptional facilities management and engineering services that exceed our clients' expectations. We are committed to delivering high quality, efficient, and reliable solutions that ensure the smooth operation of commercial and industrial spaces. We strive to foster strong relationships with our clients, built on trust, integrity, and mutual respect.

Vision Statement

Our vision is to be the leading provider of facilities management and engineering services in Ghana and beyond. We aim to set the standard for excellence in our industry, continually innovating and improving our services to meet the evolving needs of our clients. We envision a future where our work contributes significantly to the growth and success of businesses, communities, and the broader economy.
